## Enforced claims

- no intentional user-data collection;
- no analytics, cookies, advertising pixels, forms, or tracking;
- no investment advice or token-offering language;
- no audit/factual-certification overclaim;
- user and operator responsibility is explicit;
- GitHub/wallet/RPC/blockchain surfaces are identified as third-party surfaces;
- expert wallet activity remains separated from public read-only pages.

## Boundary

This package reduces and allocates risk through design, disclosure, and tests. It does not create universal legal immunity, replace counsel review, or eliminate jurisdiction-specific obligations.
