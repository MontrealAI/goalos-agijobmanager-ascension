# Third-Party and Operator Responsibility

_Last updated: 2026-06-27_

## Third parties are independent

GitHub, wallet providers, RPC providers, Etherscan, IPFS gateways, browsers, operating systems, Ethereum Mainnet, and any exchanges, bridges, custodians, or data providers are independent from this repository. Their actions, logs, outages, data practices, fees, malware, phishing, errors, and regulatory posture are not controlled by this project.

## Operators remain responsible

Operators are responsible for:

- using only public-safe data;
- protecting private keys and seed phrases;
- reviewing transaction calldata;
- confirming canonical contract addresses;
- using exact approvals rather than unlimited approvals;
- verifying gas, network, and chain ID;
- understanding irreversible blockchain risk;
- satisfying their own internal approvals;
- satisfying their own legal, tax, privacy, securities, sanctions, procurement, and compliance obligations.

## No reliance

No user may rely on the website, repository, generated docket, automated assurance result, or public presentation as legal advice, investment advice, tax advice, factual certification, audit certification, or production authorization.
