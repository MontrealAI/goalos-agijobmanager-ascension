No. Public demos have no wallet connection.

## Does it collect data?
No user data wanted; no analytics, cookies, forms, or browser storage.

## Is it an audit?
No. It is not an external audit completed claim.

## Is it AGI or ASI?
No. It does not claim achieved AGI or achieved ASI.

## What does proof-settled mean?
A claim only advances when evidence, validation, settlement boundary, and Chronicle record are present.

## What is an Evidence Docket?
A review-ready case file for a claim.

## What is a ProofBundle?
A replayable package of evidence and validation material.

## What is a Governed Decision State?
A decision plus evidence, authority, rollback posture, and claim boundary.

## Why many demos?
Each demo isolates a proof pattern without adding public wallet or data collection behavior.

## Public demos vs expert console pages?
Public demos are browser-local simulations. Expert console pages are separated and require deliberate human authority.
