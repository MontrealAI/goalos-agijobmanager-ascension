# Claim Boundary Firewall v22

A browser-local public demonstration of GoalOS claim discipline. A claim may look persuasive, but it cannot become a strong public claim until it passes source provenance, evidence support, contradiction coverage, risk classification, replay path, public/private boundary, and human review gates.

## User promise

- Choose a public-safe claim preset.
- Run the Verifier Mesh.
- See whether the claim is publishable, requires more evidence, must be quarantined, or should be rejected.
- Download a public-safe Evidence Docket JSON.

## Boundary

The page does not collect user data, does not store user input, does not connect a wallet, does not use cookies or analytics, and does not make external requests. It does not certify factual correctness. It teaches the publication rule: no Evidence Docket, no strong public claim.
