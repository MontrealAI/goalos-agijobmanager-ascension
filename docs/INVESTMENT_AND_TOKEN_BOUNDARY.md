# Investment and Token Boundary

_Last updated: 2026-06-27_

## Mandatory language

AGIALPHA references are protocol-utility and identity references only. They are not an offer, sale, recommendation, solicitation, investment contract, securities offering, derivative, investment advice, broker-dealer service, exchange service, staking product, yield product, managed account, dividend right, equity interest, debt instrument, profit-sharing instrument, or ownership claim.

## Forbidden language

Public materials must not claim or imply:

- guaranteed returns;
- expected appreciation;
- passive income;
- yield;
- dividends;
- revenue share;
- equity or ownership;
- investment suitability;
- managerial efforts for tokenholder profit;
- buy/sell/hold recommendations;
- regulatory approval;
- external audit completion unless a real independent audit exists;
- tokenholder rights in MontrealAI, QUEBEC.AI, GoalOS, AGI ALPHA, or any entity.

## Operator responsibility

Any person who acquires, holds, transfers, approves, stakes, spends, or otherwise uses any token is solely responsible for legal, tax, securities, sanctions, consumer, accounting, custody, wallet, and jurisdictional review.
